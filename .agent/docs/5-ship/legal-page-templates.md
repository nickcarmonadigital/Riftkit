## 2. Privacy Policy

```

explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

- Account data: name, email address, password (hashed)
- Billing data: payment method (processed by Stripe; we do not store card numbers)

- Content data: data you upload or create within the Service

Information Collected Automatically:
- Log data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps
- Device data: operating system, screen resolution
- Cookies: session cookies, preference cookies, analytics cookies

- Service providers: hosting (e.g., AWS), payment (Stripe), analytics (PostHog),

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account deletion,
```

## 3. Cookie Policy

```
COOKIE POLICY

Last updated: [EFFECTIVE_DATE]

This Cookie Policy explains how [COMPANY_NAME] uses cookies and similar
technologies on [APP_NAME].

1. WHAT ARE COOKIES

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website.
They help the site remember your preferences and understand how you use it.

2. COOKIES WE USE

Essential Cookies (always active):
- Session cookie: maintains your login session
- CSRF token: prevents cross-site request forgery
- Cookie consent: remembers your cookie preferences

Functional Cookies (optional):
- Theme preference: light/dark mode
- Language preference
- Sidebar state

Analytics Cookies (optional):
- PostHog: usage analytics and feature adoption tracking
- Sentry: error tracking and performance monitoring

3. MANAGING COOKIES

You can manage cookies through:
- Our cookie consent banner (shown on first visit)
- Your browser settings (block or delete cookies)
- Opt-out links for specific services

Note: disabling essential cookies may prevent the Service from functioning.

4. CONTACT US

Questions about cookies? Contact [CONTACT_EMAIL].
```

## Implementation Notes

- Place each document as a page on your marketing website (e.g., Next.js pages)
- Link to all legal pages from the app footer and during signup
- Add a cookie consent banner that links to the Cookie Policy
- Store the "last updated" date and version in your CMS or directly in the page
- Schedule legal review before each major release milestone
