## AARRR Funnel (Pirate Metrics)

> **How it works**: Track users through each stage of your funnel. If a stage is weak, focus there.
> - **Acquisition**: How do users find you?
> - **Activation**: Do they have a good first experience?
> - **Retention**: Do they come back?
> - **Revenue**: Do they pay you?
> - **Referral**: Do they tell others?
